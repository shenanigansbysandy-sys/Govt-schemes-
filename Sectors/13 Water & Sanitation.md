## Key Updates 2025-26
- **Jal Jeevan Mission** currently at **~82% saturation** (~15 crore rural households)
- Mission **extended to December 2028** for 100% rural tap water coverage
- **Swachh Bharat Mission (SBM 2.0)** focuses on waste management & liquid waste treatment
