# 🆕 New Schemes Introduced in Budget 2025-26

> **Budget Speech:** February 1, 2025  
> **Union Budget FY:** 2025-26

---

## Agriculture & Rural

| Scheme | Objective | Ministry |
|--------|-----------|----------|
| [[PM Dhan-Dhaanya Krishi Yojana]] | Boost productivity in 100 Aspirational Districts (~1.7 crore farmers) | Agriculture |
| [[Rural Prosperity & Resilience Programme]] | Multi-sectoral rural development tackling underemployment | Rural Development |
| [[Mission for Aatmanirbharta in Pulses]] | Self-reliance in pulses (Urad, Tur, Masoor) | Agriculture |

## Entrepreneurship & Industry

| Scheme | Objective | Ministry |
|--------|-----------|----------|
| [[Scheme for First-time Entrepreneurs]] | Capital & capacity-building for ~5 lakh SC/ST & women entrepreneurs | Finance |
| [[Fund of Funds for Startups]] | ₹10,000 crore to support DPIIT-recognized startups | Commerce |
| [[National Manufacturing Mission]] | Enhance manufacturing capacities & future technologies | Commerce |

## Education & Skills

| Scheme | Objective | Ministry |
|--------|-----------|----------|

| [[Atal Tinkering Labs Expansion]] | Innovation labs in ~50,000 govt schools | Education |
| [[Internship Opportunity Programme]] | 1 crore youth internships over 5 years in top 500 companies | Corporate Affairs |
