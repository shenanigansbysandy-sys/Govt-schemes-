# Kisan Credit Card (KCC) Scheme

## Overview
A flagship credit scheme providing **short-term crop loans** and **working capital** to farmers, fishers, and dairy farmers at **subsidized interest rates** (4-7% per annum).

## Key Details
- **Loan Limit:** Up to ₹3-5 lakh depending on landholding (enhanced in Budget 2025-26)
- **Interest Subvention:** 2-3% interest subvention on prompt repayment
- **Coverage:** ~7.7 crore farmers, fisherfolk, and dairy farmers
- **Purpose:** Crop production, post-harvest expenses, maintenance of farm assets, fisheries & dairy operations

## Implementation Progress (as of May 2026)
| Parameter | Status |
|-----------|--------|
| Total KCCs issued | ~7.7 crore+ active accounts |
| Budget 2025 enhancement | ✅ Working capital limits increased |
| PM-KISAN linkage | ✅ Automatic KCC renewals linked to PM-KISAN database |
| Digital KCC | 🔄 Being rolled out — end-to-end digital application |

## Challenges
- **Regional disparities** — Southern states have higher KCC penetration than Eastern/NE states
- **Delayed renewals** — Many farmers face gaps due to slow renewal processes
- **Awareness gap** — Small & marginal farmers often unaware of full benefits
- **Bank branch access** — Limited banking infrastructure in remote rural areas
- **Overdue & NPA issues** — Crop failures lead to loan defaults; waiver expectations
