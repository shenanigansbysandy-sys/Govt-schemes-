# Vatsalya Scheme (Mission Vatsalya)

## Overview
An **umbrella child protection & welfare scheme** providing a comprehensive framework for **children in difficult circumstances** — orphans, street children, child labourers, children in conflict with law, victims of abuse/trafficking, and children without parental care.

## Key Components
| Component | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Statutory Bodies** | Child Welfare Committees (CWC), Juvenile Justice Boards (JJB) — national network |
| **Alternative Care** | Foster care, sponsorship, adoption (CARINGS system) |
| **Institutional Care** | Child Care Institutions (CCIs) — shelter & rehabilitation |
| **Emergency Outreach** | Child Helpline (1098) — 24x7 emergency response |
| **Rehabilitation** | Education, skill training, counseling, legal aid |
| **Prevention** | Awareness on child marriage, child labour, trafficking |

## Implementation Progress (as of May 2026)
| Parameter | Status |
|-----------|--------|
| Childline (1098) calls handled | ✅ 5+ crore calls since launch |
| Children in institutional care | ✅ 3+ lakh children in registered CCIs |
| Foster care | 🔄 50,000+ children under foster care (growing) |
| Adoption (CARINGS portal) | ✅ 10,000+ adoptions through centralized system |
| Child Welfare Committees | ✅ 700+ CWCs across districts |
| TrackChild portal | ✅ Digital tracking of missing & recovered children |

## Challenges
- **Quality of CCIs** — Many Child Care Institutions are overcrowded, understaffed, and poorly maintained
- **Rehabilitation gaps** — Children released from CCIs at 18 years lack life skills & livelihood support
- **CWC vacancies** — Child Welfare Committees have significant vacancies in many states
- **Missing children tracking** — Despite TrackChild portal, thousands of children go missing and are never traced
- **Child marriage persistence** — 15%+ girls still married before 18 despite PMMVY awareness
- **Juvenile justice backlog** — Cases of children in conflict with law face long pendency in JJBs
