# Khelo India

## Overview
A comprehensive **sports development ecosystem** covering **identification of talent** at the grassroots level, **training & scholarships** for young athletes, **sports infrastructure** development, and **competition** at national level — to build a strong Olympic pipeline.

## Key Components
| Component | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Khelo India Youth Games (KIYG)** | Annual multi-sport event for school/college athletes |
| **Talent Identification** | Standardized physical fitness tests across 8,000+ schools |
| **Khelo India Centers** | 2,000+ sports training centres across districts |
| **Khelo India Scholarships** | ₹1-5 lakh/year for identified athletes |
| **Playground Infrastructure** | ₹1 lakh per panchayat for small playgrounds |
| **Support to NSFs** | Financial support to National Sports Federations |

## Challenges
- **Grassroots to podium** — Converting school-level talent to Olympic medal standard is a long, costly process
- **Coaching quality** — Shortage of qualified coaches at district level
- **Sports science** — Lack of sports medicine, nutrition, psychology support in Khelo India Centres
- **Dropout rate** — Many talented athletes drop out due to family pressure (education focus) or poverty
- **State disparities** — Southern states & Maharashtra produce most athletes; Bihar, UP, NE lag in infrastructure
- **Federation issues** — National Sports Federations often dysfunctional — hinders athlete progression
