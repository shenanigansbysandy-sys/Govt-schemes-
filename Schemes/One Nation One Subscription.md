# One Nation One Subscription (ONOS)

## Overview
A **centralized national subscription** that provides all higher education & research institutions in India with **free access** to over **13,000 international research journals** from major publishers (Elsevier, Springer, Nature, Taylor & Francis, etc.)

## Key Details
- **Coverage:** 6,000+ government-funded institutions (universities, colleges, IITs, NITs, research labs)
- **Publishers Covered:** 30+ major international publishers
- **Journal Access:** 13,000+ journals across all disciplines
- **Budget:** ₹1,000+ crore annually (negotiated centrally — bulk discount)
- **Portal:** Access via **One Nation One Subscription portal** using institutional login

## Implementation Progress (as of May 2026)
| Parameter | Status |
|-----------|--------|
| Institutions onboarded | ✅ 5,500+ institutions activated |
| Publishers signed | ✅ 30+ publishers |
| Journals accessible | ✅ 13,000+ |
| Usage statistics | 📈 2+ crore article downloads in Year 1 |
| State universities enrolled | 🔄 ~500 more pending onboarding |

## Challenges
- **Awareness gap** — Many faculty & students in tier-2/3 institutions unaware of access
- **Bandwidth issues** — Accessing full-text articles requires good internet
- **Authenticated access** — VPN & login requirements create friction for users
- **Copyright restrictions** — No downloading/printing rights for some publishers
- **Equity concerns** — Better-resourced institutions already had subscriptions; this helps smaller ones more
