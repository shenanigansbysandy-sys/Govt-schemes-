# PM Jan Dhan Yojana (PMJDY)

## Overview
National financial inclusion mission providing **universal banking access** to all Indian citizens with zero-balance accounts.

## Key Features
- **Zero balance** savings bank account
- **RuPay debit card** with inbuilt accident insurance cover (₹2 lakh)
- **Overdraft facility** up to ₹10,000
- **Direct Benefit Transfer** (DBT) linkage
- **Mobile banking** facility

## Key Stats
- **50+ crore** bank accounts opened
- **55%+** accounts belong to women
- **67%+** accounts in rural/semi-urban areas
