# PM MITRA (Mega Integrated Textile Regions & Apparel)

## Overview
A scheme to establish **7 Mega Textile Parks** across India with **world-class industrial infrastructure** (plug-and-play) to attract investment, boost textile manufacturing, and create **large-scale employment**.

## Key Details
- **7 Parks approved** in: Tamil Nadu, Punjab, Gujarat, Maharashtra, Karnataka, Madhya Pradesh, Uttar Pradesh
- **Ecosystem:** Spinning → weaving → processing → garmenting → printing → packaging (all under one roof)
- **Infrastructure:** Common effluent treatment, power substation, water supply, logistics hub, labour housing
- **Land:** 1,000+ acres per park
- **Investment Target:** ₹10,000+ crore per park (private + government)

## Challenges
- **Land acquisition delays** — 2 parks facing legal/administrative hurdles in land allotment
- **Global textile slump** — Weakening global demand for textiles impacts investment appetite
- **China competition** — Bangladesh, Vietnam, China dominate global textiles with better cost structures
- **Cotton price volatility** — Unpredictable cotton prices affect textile profitability
- **Environmental clearances** — Common effluent treatment plants need forest & pollution clearances
