# Anusandhan National Research Foundation (ANRF)

## Overview
India's **apex research funding body**, established through the ANRF Act 2023, to **seed, grow, and promote** research and development across universities, colleges, and research institutions in all fields (S&T, social sciences, humanities).

## Key Details
- **Structure:** Government + Industry co-funding model (₹50,000 crore corpus over 5 years)
- **Ministries Involved:** Ministry of Education + DST + DBT + MoHFW + Industry
- **Focus Areas:** 
  1. Basic & applied research
  2. Translational research (lab to market)
  3. Research infrastructure in universities
  4. Mission-mode projects (climate, health, AI, quantum)
- **Key Initiatives:** 
  - **National Quantum Mission** (sub-component)
  - **Deep Ocean Mission** linkage
  - **AI/ML research hubs**

## Challenges
- **Industry funding gap** — Private sector contribution lower than expected
- **University research culture** — Teaching-heavy focus; limited research orientation in most universities
- **Bureaucratic hurdles** — Multi-ministry coordination slows down fund disbursement
- **Brain drain** — Top researchers still prefer foreign institutions over Indian universities
- **Patenting & commercialization** — Weak IP culture; few university patents translate to products
