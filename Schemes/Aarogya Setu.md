# Aarogya Setu

## Overview
India's **official COVID-19 contact tracing & health management app** — initially used for exposure alerts, self-assessment, and lab test booking. Post-pandemic, it has been **repurposed as a general public health app** with health services integration.

## Key Details
- **Original Purpose (2020-2022):** Contact tracing via Bluetooth + GPS, COVID self-assessment
- **COVID Usage:** 20+ crore downloads, helped in early detection & containment
- **Post-COVID Evolution:** 
  - **Health record access** — ABHA (Ayushman Bharat Health Account) linkage
  - **Vaccination certificate** — Digital COVID vaccine certificate (CoWIN integration)
  - **Tele-consultation** — eSanjeevani linkages
  - **Health Advisory** — Personalized health tips, seasonal disease alerts
  - **Organ donation pledge** — Digital pledge

## Implementation Progress (as of May 2026)
| Parameter | Status |
|-----------|--------|
| Total downloads | ✅ 20+ crore |
| ABHA integration | ✅ 5+ crore health accounts linked |
| Monthly active users | 📉 Reduced significantly post-COVID (~2 crore/month) |
| Vaccine certificate access | ✅ Integrated |
| eSanjeevani tele-consult | 🔄 Being integrated |
| General health surveys | 🔄 Seasonal disease surveillance features added |
| International adoption | ✅ Open-sourced — 100+ countries used the technology |

## Challenges
- **Privacy concerns** — Initially faced criticism for data collection, lack of sunset clause
- **Post-COVID relevance** — App usage declined sharply; ongoing value proposition unclear
- **Feature bloat** — Trying to do too many things (health, COVID, insurance, etc.)
- **Not mandatory post-COVID** — Most users have uninstalled; re-adoption is low
- **Google-Apple API dependency** — Contact tracing initially didn't use GAEN framework (privacy issue)
