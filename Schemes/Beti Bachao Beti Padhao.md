# Beti Bachao Beti Padhao (BBBP)

## Overview
A landmark scheme addressing the **declining Child Sex Ratio (CSR)** and promoting the **education, survival, and empowerment of the girl child**. Launched by PM Modi on Jan 22, 2015 in Panipat, Haryana.

## Three Pillars
| Pillar | Focus | Lead Ministry |
|--------|-------|---------------|
| **Survival** | Preventing sex-selective abortion, improving girl child health | Health & FW |
| **Protection** | Preventing child marriage, child labour, trafficking | WCD |
| **Education** | Enrolment & retention of girls in school, higher education | Education |

## Key Metrics
| Indicator | 2014-15 | 2025-26 | Trend |
|-----------|---------|---------|-------|
| Child Sex Ratio (0-6 yrs) | 918 | ~930 | ✅ Improving |
| Sex Ratio at Birth | 898 | ~910 | ✅ Improving |
| Girls enrolment (secondary) | 78% | 88% | ✅ Improved |
| Girls dropout (secondary) | 17% | 12% | ✅ Reduced |
| Child marriage incidence | 27% | ~15% | ✅ Declining |

## Implementation Progress (as of May 2026)
| Parameter | Status |
|-----------|--------|
| Low CSR districts covered | ✅ 640+ districts |
| PC&PNDT Act enforcement | 🔄 Improved but sex-selective abortion persists |
| School enrolment campaigns | ✅ Balika Manch, Kanya Udyan in schools |
| Community outreach | ✅ Massive awareness — BBBP is widely recognized brand |
| Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana linkage | ✅ Girls get financial accounts under SSY |

## Challenges
- **Deep-rooted son preference** — Social norm change is generational; still strong in Haryana, UP, Rajasthan, Punjab
- **PC&PNDT Act underenforcement** — Few convictions despite illegal clinics operating
- **Ultrasound access** — Portable ultrasound machines made sex-detection easier and harder to regulate
- **School dropout still high** — Girls still drop out post-Class 10 due to early marriage, household work, safety concerns
- **Only 2-child norm linkage feared** — Some families see BBBP as incentivizing 2-child norm (which hurts girls)
