# Bharatmala Pariyojana

## Overview
India's **largest highway development program**, aiming to optimize the efficiency of road transportation across the country through **34,800+ km of National Highway** construction/upgradation.

## Key Components
| Component | Length (km) | Focus |
|-----------|-------------|-------|
| Economic Corridors | 9,000+ | High-traffic freight routes |
| Inter-corridor & Feeder Roads | 6,000+ | Connecting economic corridors |
| National Corridor Efficiency | 5,000+ | Widening of existing Golden Quadrilateral & NS-EW corridors |
| Border Road Development | 3,600+ | Strategic roads along borders |
| Coastal & Port Connectivity | 2,000+ | Port-to-hinterland roads |
| Expressways | 1,800+ | Greenfield high-speed access-controlled highways |

## Implementation Progress (as of May 2026)
| Parameter | Status |
|-----------|--------|
| Total length awarded | ✅ 34,800+ km |
| Completed | ✅ 20,000+ km |
| Under construction | 🔄 12,000+ km |
| Balance (to be awarded) | 🔄 ~2,800 km |
| Expressways completed | ✅ 5 Greenfield expressways (Delhi-Meerut, Delhi-Mumbai, etc.) |

## Challenges
- **Land acquisition delays** — Biggest bottleneck; courts, farmer protests, compensation disputes
- **Cost overruns** — Many projects have 30-50% cost escalation due to delays
- **Environmental clearance** — Forest & wildlife clearances cause significant delays
- **Contractor capacity** — Some contractors lack technical & financial capacity for mega projects
- **Maintenance quality** — Rapidly built highways sometimes develop potholes within 2-3 years
