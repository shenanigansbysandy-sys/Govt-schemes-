# Setu Bhartam Yojana

## Overview
A scheme focused on **construction of road-over-bridges (ROBs), road-under-bridges (RUBs), and missing bridges** on National Highways to eliminate **level crossings** and improve **road safety & connectivity**.

## Key Details
- **Primary Objective:** Eliminate all unmanned level crossings on National Highways
- **Components:**
  1. Conversion of level crossings to ROBs/RUBs
  2. Widening & strengthening of existing bridges
  3. New bridges on missing links
  4. Flood-damaged bridge restoration
- **Safety Impact:** Level crossings cause 30-40% of rail-related deaths

## Implementation Progress (as of May 2026)
| Parameter | Status |
|-----------|--------|
| Unmanned level crossings eliminated | ✅ 10,000+ eliminated (all on NHs) |
| ROBs/RUBs constructed | ✅ 3,000+ |
| Bridges widened/strengthened | 🔄 5,000+ completed |
| New bridges (missing links) | ✅ 500+ |
| Rail accidents at level crossings | 📈 Reduced by 80%+ |

## Challenges
- **High cost** — A single ROB costs ₹30-100 crore depending on span
- **Railway coordination** — Requires coordination with railways for rail traffic blocks during construction
- **Land acquisition** — Approach roads for ROBs require additional land
- **Flood damage** — Bridges in flood-prone areas require frequent repair
- **Maintenance** — Existing bridges built decades ago need regular strengthening
