# PM Swasthya Suraksha Yojna (PMSSY)

## Overview
Launched to **correct regional imbalances** in affordable & reliable tertiary healthcare, PMSSY establishes **AIIMS-like institutions** and **upgrades existing government medical colleges** across India.

## Key Components
| Component | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **New AIIMS** | 22 new AIIMS established/under construction (from original 6) |
| **Medical College Upgradation** | 75+ government medical colleges upgraded with super-specialty blocks |
| **District Hospitals** | Strengthening district hospitals with critical care |

## Implementation Progress (as of May 2026)
| Parameter | Status |
|-----------|--------|
| New AIIMS operational | ✅ 14 of 22 AIIMS fully functional |
| AIIMS under construction | 🔄 Remaining 8 in various stages |
| Medical colleges upgraded | ✅ 75+ completed |
| New MBBS seats created | ✅ 10,000+ additional MBBS seats |
| Patient footfall | 📈 2+ crore OPD/year across new AIIMS |

## Challenges
- **Construction delays** — Several AIIMS (e.g., AIIMS Rajkot, Bathinda) face significant construction delays
- **Faculty recruitment** — Difficulty attracting doctors to newly established AIIMS in smaller cities
- **Cost overruns** — Original cost estimates have doubled/tripled for many projects
- **Regional disparities persist** — North-Eastern states and remote areas still underserved
- **Quality concerns** — New AIIMS not yet matching the standard of the original AIIMS Delhi
