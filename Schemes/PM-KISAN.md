# PM-KISAN (PM Kisan Samman Nidhi)

## Overview
Income support scheme providing **₹6,000 per year** to all land-holding farmers in India, paid in three 4-monthly instalments of ₹2,000 each.

## Key Details
- **Benefit:** ₹6,000/year direct cash transfer to bank accounts
- **Beneficiaries:** ~11 crore+ farmers
- **22nd Instalment:** Released February 2026
- **Budget 2026:** Proposed hikes for certain clusters

## Eligibility
- All farmers with cultivable land
- Excludes institutional landholders and income tax payers

## How to Apply
1. Visit https://pmkisan.gov.in
2. Click "New Farmer Registration"
3. Enter Aadhaar, bank details, and land records
4. Track status via "Beneficiary Status" tab
