# Swachh Bharat Mission (SBM)

## Overview
India's largest cleanliness and sanitation mission, aiming to achieve **open-defecation free (ODF) India** and complete sanitation coverage.

## Milestones
- **SBM 1.0 (2014-2019):** Declared India ODF; built **10+ crore toilets**
- **SBM 2.0 (2020-2025+):** Focus on **waste management**, liquid waste treatment, faecal sludge management, plastic waste management
- **Gandhi Jayanti** (Oct 2) drives annual campaigns

## Key Components
- Individual household toilets (IHHL)
- Community & public toilets
- Solid & liquid waste management
- Behaviour change communication (Swachhagraha)
- **Swachh Sarvekshan** — Annual cleanliness rankings of cities & districts
