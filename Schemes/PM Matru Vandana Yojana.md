# PM Matru Vandana Yojana (PMMVY)

## Overview
A **Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT)** scheme providing **₹5,000** in cash to pregnant & lactating women (19+ years) for their **first live birth**, to compensate for wage loss and ensure proper nutrition & health during pregnancy.

## Key Details
- **Benefit:** ₹5,000 in three instalments (conditional on health milestones)
  - Instalment 1: Early pregnancy registration (₹1,000)
  - Instalment 2: After 6 months pregnancy + 1 ANC checkup (₹2,000)
  - Instalment 3: Childbirth registration + 1st vaccination cycle (₹2,000)
- **Total Benefit (with JSY):** Combined PMMVY + JSY benefit ~₹6,000 per pregnancy
- **Eligibility:** First live birth, woman aged 19+ years
- **Mode:** Direct transfer to Aadhaar-linked bank account

## Implementation Progress (as of May 2026)
| Parameter | Status |
|-----------|--------|
| Beneficiaries covered | ✅ 3+ crore women |
| Amount transferred | ✅ ₹15,000+ crore |
| Coverage among eligible | 🔄 ~65% — many eligible women not enrolled |
| JSY + PMMVY convergence | 🔄 Being streamlined — single disbursement |

## Challenges
- **Coverage gap** — ~35% eligible women not covered; mostly in remote areas
- **Delay in instalments** — Conditional milestones cause delays; many women get full amount only post-delivery
- **Aadhaar issues** — Biometric failure, no Aadhaar, or non-linked bank accounts cause exclusion
- **JSY overlap** — Confusion between JSY (Janani Suraksha Yojana) & PMMVY benefits
- **Bank account access** — Many rural women don't have independent bank accounts
- **First birth only** — Second & third children excluded, though many families need continued support
