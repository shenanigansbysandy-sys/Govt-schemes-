# Mission LiFE (Lifestyle for Environment)

## Overview
A global mass-movement introduced by India at the **2022 UN Climate Change Conference (COP27)** to promote **environmentally conscious lifestyle choices** — making climate action a people-led movement rather than government-led.

## Key Details
- **Concept:** *"Reduce, Reuse, Recycle & Re-purpose"* — individual & community actions
- **7 Themes:** 
  1. Save Water (tap management, rain harvesting)
  2. Save Energy (LED bulbs, efficient appliances)
  3. Reduce Waste (segregation, composting)
  4. Reduce E-Waste
  5. Say No to Single-Use Plastic
  6. Adopt Sustainable Food Systems (millets, local food)
  7. Healthy Lifestyles (walking, cycling)
- **Global dimension:** India taking the lead — other countries adopting the model
- **LiFE Score:** Citizens earn points for pro-environment actions

## Implementation Progress (as of May 2026)
| Parameter | Status |
|-----------|--------|
| LiFE portal registrations | ✅ 1+ crore citizens registered |
| LiFE Ambassadors | ✅ 1+ lakh individuals trained as promoters |

| Global adoption | ✅ 15+ countries adopted LiFE framework |

## Challenges
- **Behavioural change** — Moving from awareness to sustained action is difficult
- **Greenwashing** — Some corporations using LiFE branding without genuine change
- **Measurement** — Hard to quantify the actual environmental impact of lifestyle changes
- **Equity concerns** — Low-income groups may not have the means to "choose green"
- **Policy gaps** — LiFE remains voluntary; no regulatory teeth
