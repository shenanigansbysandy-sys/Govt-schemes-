# PM Awas Yojana (PMAY)

## Overview
Flagship housing scheme aiming to provide **"Housing for All"** with financial assistance for construction/purchase of houses for urban and rural poor.

## PMAY 2.0 (Launched Late 2024)
- Building an additional **3 crore houses** for middle & rural families
- Enhanced subsidy amounts
- Focus on **middle-income groups** (MIG)

## Components
| Component | Beneficiary |
|-----------|-------------|
| PMAY-Urban | Urban poor, slum dwellers |
| PMAY-Gramin | Rural landless & poor |
| PMAY-MIG | Middle-income groups (2.0) |

## Subsidy
- Up to **₹2.67 lakh** per house (Urban)
- Up to **₹1.20 lakh** per house (Rural)
- Additional **MIG subsidy** in 2.0 phase
