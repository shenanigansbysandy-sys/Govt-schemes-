# Lakhpati Didi Scheme

## Overview
A transformative women's empowerment scheme under **DAY-NRLM** that aims to train women in **Self Help Groups (SHGs)** to earn **₹1,00,000+ per annum** through skill training, enterprise development, and market linkages.

## Key Details
- **Original Target (2023):** 3 crore Lakhpati Didis by March 2028
- **Revised Target (Feb 2026):** ✅ **Doubled to 6 crore** by March 2029
- **How it works:** 
  1. Women formed into SHGs → community bonding & savings
  2. Skill training in 50+ livelihood areas
  3. Bank linkage for enterprise loans
  4. Market access through digital platforms & government procurement
- **Sectors:** Food processing, tailoring, handicrafts, dairy, poultry, solar, banking correspondent

## Implementation Progress (as of May 2026)
| Parameter | Status |
|-----------|--------|
| Women in SHGs | ✅ 9+ crore women in 80+ lakh SHGs |
| Lakhpati Didis achieved | ✅ ~1.5 crore women earning ₹1L+/year |
| Bank credit linked | ✅ ₹6+ lakh crore disbursed to SHGs |
| Skill training provided | ✅ 2+ crore women trained |
| Rural livelihood enterprises | ✅ 50+ lakh women-led micro-enterprises |

## Challenges
- **Skill gaps** — Many women lack basic numerical & digital literacy for enterprise management
- **Market access** — Finding consistent markets for SHG products is a major hurdle
- **Over-indebtedness** — Some families take multiple loans, leading to debt traps
- **Patriarchal barriers** — Women's control over earnings is limited in many households
- **Bank linkage** — Banks often reluctant to lend without collateral despite CGTMSE
