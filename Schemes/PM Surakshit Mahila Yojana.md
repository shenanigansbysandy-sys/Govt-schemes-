# PM Surakshit Mahila Yojana

## Overview
A new comprehensive scheme announced in **Budget 2025-26** to provide **safety support, 24x7 helpline, legal aid, and emergency response** for women facing violence, harassment, or security threats.

## Key Details
- **Components:**
  1. **24x7 Helpline** (181 — existing, strengthened) — emergency response & counselling
  2. **One-Stop Centres (OSCs)** — Integrated support in every district (medical, legal, police, shelter)
  3. **Legal Aid** — Free legal assistance through empanelled lawyers
  4. **Emergency Shelters** — Short-term accommodation for women in distress
  5. **Digital Safety** — Cyber harassment & stalking reporting mechanism
  6. **Awareness** — School & college-level gender sensitization programmes
- **Integration:** Linkage with Nirbhaya Fund, One-Stop Centres, and state police systems

| Digital safety portal | 🔄 Being developed — beta expected Q3 2026 |

## Challenges
- **Police sensitization** — FIR registration for women's complaints remains low in many states
- **Conviction rates** — Very low conviction rates in crimes against women discourage reporting
- **Stigma & social pressure** — Many women don't report domestic violence due to family pressure
- **Shelter quality** — Existing government shelters have poor conditions & capacity constraints
- **Awareness** — Many women unaware of helpline (181) & One-Stop Centre services
