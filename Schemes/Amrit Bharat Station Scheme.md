# Amrit Bharat Station Scheme

## Overview
A massive **railway station redevelopment program** to transform **1,300+ railway stations** across India into **world-class transit hubs** with modern amenities, improved accessibility, and integrated multimodal transport.

## Key Features
- **Coverage:** 1,300+ stations across all zones & categories
- **Redevelopment Includes:**
  - Modern station buildings (airport-like concourses)
  - Platform improvements (covered, wider, foot overbridges)
  - Passenger amenities (waiting rooms, clean toilets, lifts/escalators)
  - Divyang-friendly facilities
  - Multimodal integration (bus, auto, metro, taxi)
  - Wi-Fi, digital displays, better signage
- **Funding:** Station redevelopment through PPP model + Railway budget

## Implementation Progress (as of May 2026)

|-----------|--------|

| Completed | ✅ 50+ stations redeveloped (e.g., Rani Kamlapati, Gandhinagar) |

## Challenges
- **Heritage concerns** — Many stations have heritage status (e.g., CSMT, Howrah) — redevelopment must preserve heritage
- **Railway operations disruption** — Construction while maintaining train operations is complex
- **PPP viability** — Many station redevelopment PPP bids have received poor response
- **Land encroachment** — Stations are surrounded by encroachments requiring rehabilitation
- **Funding gap** — Budget allocation insufficient for all 1,300 stations simultaneously
