# PM Suraksha Bima Yojana (PMSBY)

## Overview
A **low-cost accidental insurance scheme** offering **₹2 lakh cover for accidental death & permanent total disability** and **₹1 lakh for permanent partial disability** at a nominal annual premium of **₹20**.

## Key Details
- **Cover:**
  - Accidental death: ₹2 lakh
  - Permanent total disability: ₹2 lakh
  - Permanent partial disability: ₹1 lakh
- **Premium:** ₹20 per year (auto-debit from savings bank account)
- **Eligibility:** Savings bank account holder, age 18-70 years
- **Tenure:** 1 year (renewable annually)
- **Enrolments:** 30+ crore (largest accident insurance scheme globally)

## Benefits
- Ultra-low premium (less than ₹2/month)
- No medical checkup
- Covers death due to road accident, drowning, burns, falls, etc.
- Auto-debit ensures continuity (if balance sufficient)

## Challenges
- **Claim awareness** — Families often don't know deceased had PMSBY cover
- **Claim documentation** — Requires FIR, post-mortem, death certificate — rural families struggle
- **Exclusions not well communicated** - Certain activities (adventure sports, self-harm) excluded
- **Low claim ratio** - Only 30-40% of eligible deaths result in claim filing
- **Auto-debit churn** - Policies lapsed due to low balance; re-enrolment required
