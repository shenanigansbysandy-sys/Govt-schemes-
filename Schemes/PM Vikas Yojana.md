# PM Vikas Yojana

## Overview
A comprehensive **welfare scheme for backward and minority communities** (Muslims, Christians, Sikhs, Buddhists, Jains, Parsis) focusing on **education, skill development, entrepreneurship, and infrastructure** in minority-concentrated areas.

## Key Components
| Component | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Education** | Scholarships (pre-matric, post-matric, merit-cum-means) for minority students |
| **Skill Development** | 'Seekho aur Kamao' (Learn & Earn) — job-linked skill training |
| **Entrepreneurship** | Credit support through Mudra, Stand Up India linkage |
| **Infrastructure** | Multi-purpose community centres, schools, hostels in minority-concentrated areas |
| **Women Empowerment** | 'Nai Roshni' — leadership training for minority women |
| **UPSC Coaching** | Free coaching for minority students appearing in competitive exams |

## Implementation Progress (as of May 2026)
| Parameter | Status |
|-----------|--------|
| Minority-concentrated districts | ✅ 120+ districts identified |
| Scholarships disbursed | ✅ 3+ crore students assisted (cumulative) |
| Skill training (Seekho aur Kamao) | ✅ 5+ lakh youth trained |
| Placement-linked training | ✅ ~65% placement rate |
| Infrastructure projects | ✅ 2,000+ community centres/hostels built |
| Nai Roshni (women leadership) | ✅ 5+ lakh women trained |

## Challenges
- **Underutilization of funds** — Ministry of Minority Affairs consistently underspends its budget
- **Scholarship disbursal delays** — Students often get scholarships months after the academic year starts
- **Targeting issues** — Some minoritycommunity members (caste within Muslims/Christians) are more backward than others — broad-brush approach misses them
- **Political sensitivity** — Minority welfare is politically charged — some states don't effectively implement
- **Quality of skill training** — Many training centres use outdated curriculum; jobs secured are low-paying
