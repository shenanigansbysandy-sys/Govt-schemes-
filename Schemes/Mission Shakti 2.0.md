# Mission Shakti 2.0

## Overview
A comprehensive **women's empowerment umbrella scheme** launched in 2025, consolidating & revamping multiple existing women-focused schemes (Swadhar, Ujjawala, Working Women Hostel, etc.) into a single integrated framework.

## Key Details
- **Two Wings:**
  1. **Sambal (Safety & Security):**
     - One-Stop Centres, helpline (181), women police volunteers
     - Anti-trafficking (Ujjawala), shelter homes (Swadhar Greh)
     - Cyber crime against women response
  2. **Samarthya (Empowerment):**
     - Skill training & livelihood for women
     - Working women hostel
     - Gender budgeting & mainstreaming
     - Awareness & advocacy

## Implementation Progress (as of May 2026)
| Parameter | Status |
|-----------|--------|

| Anti-trafficking | 🔄 200+ Ujjawala rehabilitation centres |
| Gender budgeting | 🔄 All ministries now required to submit gender budget |

## Challenges
- **Merger complexity** — Merging 5+ schemes with different rules, funding patterns, and state-level structures is challenging
- **Inadequate shelter homes** — Existing homes have poor quality, understaffing issues
- **Low conviction for crimes** — Even with good infrastructure, conviction rates remain low
- **Budget adequacy** — Consolidated scheme doesn't necessarily mean enhanced budget
- **State capacity** — Women & Child Development departments in states are among the weakest administratively
