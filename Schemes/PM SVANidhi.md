# PM SVANidhi

## Overview
A **micro-credit scheme** for **street vendors** who were impacted by COVID-19, providing collateral-free working capital loans of up to **₹10,000** (with interest subsidy), with an incentive for digital transactions.

## Key Details
- **Loan Tiers:** ₹10,000 → ₹20,000 → ₹50,000 (subsequent rounds for prompt repayment)
- **Interest Subsidy:** 7% interest subvention (effective ~0-2% interest)
- **Cashback:** ₹100-₹750/month cashback for digital transactions
- **Eligibility:** Street vendors with Certificate of Vending (or in Town Vending Committee list)

## Implementation Progress (as of May 2026)
| Parameter | Status |
|-----------|--------|
| Total loan sanctioned | ✅ 60+ lakh loans disbursed |
| Cumulative value | ✅ ₹10,000+ crore disbursed |
| Repeat borrowers (Tier 2/3) | ✅ ~30 lakh vendors upgraded |
| Women beneficiaries | ✅ ~45% of total beneficiaries |
| Digital transactions incentivized | ✅ ₹500+ crore cashback disbursed |

## Challenges
- **Identification gaps** — Many genuine street vendors don't have Certificate of Vending
- **Loan recovery** — Some vendors defaulted during economic downturns
- **Limited loan size** — ₹10,000-₹50,000 may be insufficient for capital-intensive vending
- **Bank reluctance** — Some banks are hesitant to lend without collateral
- **Municipal harassment** — Vendors face eviction & harassment despite scheme coverage
