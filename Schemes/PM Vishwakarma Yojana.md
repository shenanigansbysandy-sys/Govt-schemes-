# PM Vishwakarma Yojana

## Overview
Holistic support scheme for **traditional artisans and craftspeople** working with their hands and tools across 18 identified trades. Provides skill enhancement, toolkit support, and credit access.

## 18 Covered Trades
Carpenter, Blacksmith, Hammer & Tool Kit Maker, Potter, Sculptor, Stone Carver, Mason, Basket/Mat/Broom Maker, Doll & Toy Maker, Barber, Garland Maker, Washerman, Tailor, Fishing Net Repairer, Armourer, Cauhari, Teli, and more.

## Benefits (Phase II)
| Benefit | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Toolkit E-Voucher** | ₹15,000 value |
| **Loans** | Up to ₹3 lakh at **5% interest** (collateral-free) |
| **Skill Training** | Stipend of ₹500/day during training |
| **Digital Enablement** | Udyam registration, digital payments, QR code |

## Eligibility
- Traditional artisans aged 18+ engaged in covered trades
- Not a government employee or income tax payer
