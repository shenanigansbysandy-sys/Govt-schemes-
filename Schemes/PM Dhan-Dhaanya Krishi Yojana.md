# PM Dhan-Dhaanya Krishi Yojana

## Overview
Launched in **Budget 2025-26**, this scheme aims to improve **agricultural productivity, crop diversification, storage infrastructure, and irrigation facilities** in **100 Aspirational Districts** across India. It targets approximately **1.7 crore farmers**.

## Key Details
- **Target:** 100 Aspirational Districts with low agricultural productivity
- **Focus Areas:** Productivity enhancement, crop diversification, post-harvest storage, irrigation
- **Beneficiaries:** ~1.7 crore small & marginal farmers

## Implementation Progress (as of May 2026)
| Parameter | Status |
|-----------|--------|
| District Identification | ✅ Completed — 100 Aspirational Districts selected |
| Program Framework | ✅ Finalized |
| Ground-level rollout | 🔄 Underway in select districts |
| Convergence with PM-KISAN | 🔄 Being integrated |

## Challenges
- **Coordination complexity** — Requires convergence across multiple ministries (Agriculture, Water, Rural Development)
- **Varied agro-climatic conditions** — A one-size-fits-all approach won't work across 100 districts
- **Last-mile delivery** — Reaching small & marginal farmers in remote areas
- **Data gaps** — Accurate beneficiary identification in aspirational districts remains a challenge
