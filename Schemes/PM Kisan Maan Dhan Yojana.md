# PM Kisan Maan Dhan Yojana (PM-KMDY)

## Overview
A **voluntary pension scheme** for small and marginal farmers that provides a **minimum monthly pension of ₹3,000** after attaining 60 years of age.

## Key Details
- **Eligibility:** Small & marginal farmers (up to 2 hectares) aged 18-40 years
- **Contribution:** 
  - Farmer contributes ₹55-₹200/month (varies by entry age)
  - Central Government contributes matching amount
- **Pension:** ₹3,000/month after 60 years
- **Family pension:** 50% of pension to spouse after farmer's death
- **Enrolment:** ~25 lakh+ farmers enrolled so far

## Implementation Progress (as of May 2026)
| Parameter | Status |
|-----------|--------|
| Total enrolments | ✅ ~25 lakh+ |
| Target | 👁️ 5+ crore farmers |
| PM-KISAN auto-enrolment | 🔄 Being piloted — auto-enrolment via PM-KISAN database |
| Awareness campaigns | 🔄 Expanded through Common Service Centres (CSCs) |

## Challenges
- **Very low enrolment** — Only ~25 lakh out of a target of 5+ crore (0.5% achievement)
- **Awareness gap** — Majority of small farmers are unaware of the scheme
- **Trust deficit** — Farmers are skeptical about government pension schemes (past experiences)
- **Affordability** — Even ₹55-200/month is seen as burdensome by marginal farmers
- **Complexity** — Multiple pension schemes (PM-KMDY, PM-SYM, APY) create confusion
