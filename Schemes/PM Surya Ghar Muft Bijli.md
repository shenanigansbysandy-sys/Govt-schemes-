# PM Surya Ghar: Muft Bijli Yojana

## Overview
Provides **300 units of free electricity per month** to 1 crore households through rooftop solar installations by 2027.

## Key Features
- **Subsidy:** Up to **₹78,000 per household**
- **Benefit:** Free electricity + sell surplus to grid
- **Target:** 1 crore households by 2027
- **Milestone:** Crossed **10 lakh rooftop solar installations** (world's largest residential solar scheme)

## Eligibility
- Indian residential households
- Own roof suitable for solar panel installation
- No income bar

## How to Apply
1. Visit https://pmsuryaghar.gov.in
2. Register with electricity consumer number and mobile
3. Get feasibility approval from DISCOM
4. Install through registered vendors
5. Subsidy credited to bank account within 30 days
