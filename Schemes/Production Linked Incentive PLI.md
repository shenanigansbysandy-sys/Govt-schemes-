# Production Linked Incentive (PLI) Scheme

## Overview
India's most ambitious industrial policy intervention — offering **incremental production-linked incentives** (4-6% of sales) to companies in **14+ strategic sectors** to boost domestic manufacturing, create jobs, and reduce import dependence.

## PLI 2.0 (2025) — Expanded
- **New sectors:** E-vehicles (deeper), semiconductors, green hydrogen, AI hardware, space
- **Enhanced incentives** for sunrise sectors
- **MSME-friendly** — lower eligibility thresholds for small units

## Challenges
- **Complex eligibility criteria** — MSMEs struggle to meet investment/sales thresholds
- **Delayed disbursement** — Many companies complain of slow incentive processing
- **Component import dependence** — PLI boosted assembly but component manufacturing still imports (especially China)
- **China dependency** — Electronics & solar PLI still depend on Chinese intermediates
- **Dispute resolution** — No clear mechanism for incentive disputes between companies & government
