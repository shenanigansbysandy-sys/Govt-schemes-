# UMANG (Unified Mobile Application for New-age Governance)

## Overview
A **single mobile app** providing access to **300+ government services** from Central, State, and local bodies — ranging from Aadhaar services to passport applications, EPFO, and income tax.

## Key Details
- **Platforms:** iOS, Android, Web
- **Services:** 300+ government services across 40+ departments
- **Featured Services:** 
  - Aadhaar services (download, lock/unlock, verify)
  - EPFO (PF balance, claims)
  - DigiLocker (access documents)
  - Passport Seva
  - Income Tax (ITR filing, PAN)
  - Driving License & RC
  - Gas booking (PAHAL/DBT)
  - PM-KISAN beneficiary search
  - Ayushman Bharat (e-card)
- **Languages:** 13 Indian languages + English
- **Users:** 5+ crore registered users

## Challenges
- **Awareness gap** — Majority of Indian smartphone users unaware of UMANG
- **Service quality variance** — Some departments' services are poorly integrated (broken links, errors)
- **Authentication friction** — OTP-based login works well but first-time setup can be confusing
- **State services gap** — Many state-level services not yet integrated
- **Internet dependency** — App requires constant internet; offline mode limited
