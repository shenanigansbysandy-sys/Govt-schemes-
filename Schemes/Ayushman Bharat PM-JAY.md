# Ayushman Bharat (PM-JAY)

## Overview
World's largest health insurance scheme providing **₹5 lakh per family per year** for secondary and tertiary care hospitalization.

## Key Expansion (2025-26)
- **Universal Senior Cover:** All citizens **aged 70+** now covered via **"Vay Vandana" cards**
- **Ayushman Arogya Mandir:** Wellness centres integrated with the scheme in villages

## Coverage
- **Hospitalization:** Pre & post hospitalization expenses covered
- **No cap on family size** or age of family members
- **Portable** across India (cashless anywhere)

## Eligibility
- Low-income families based on Socio-Economic Caste Census (SECC) 2011 data
- **NEW:** All citizens aged 70+ (regardless of income)
