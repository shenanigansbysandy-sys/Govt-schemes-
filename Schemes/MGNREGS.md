# MGNREGS (Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Scheme)

## Overview
India's largest social security scheme providing **100 days of guaranteed wage employment** per financial year to every rural household whose adult members volunteer to do unskilled manual work.

## Key Features
- **100 days guaranteed employment** per household per year
- **Legal right to employment** (enforceable through Gram Panchayats)
- **Equal wages** for men and women
- **Social audit** mechanism for transparency
- **Asset creation** (water conservation, rural connectivity, land development)

## Wage Rate
- Varies by state (range: ₹230–₹380 per day in FY2026)
- **Minimum 33% women participation** (often exceeds 50%)

## Key Stats
- **1.11 lakh crore budget** allocation
- Covers **all rural districts** across India
- **Drought & disaster-proofing** through water conservation works
