# Samagra Shiksha 2.0

## Overview
An **integrated school education scheme** from pre-primary to Class 12, subsuming Sarva Shiksha Abhiyan (SSA), Rashtriya Madhyamik Shiksha Abhiyan (RMSA), and Teacher Education (TE) programs. **Samagra Shiksha 2.0** (2025) adds a strong **EdTech & digital learning** focus.

## Key Details
- **Coverage:** 16+ lakh schools, 1.5+ lakh teachers, 26+ crore students
- **Components:** 
  - Infrastructure & facilities (toilets, electricity, labs, smart classrooms)
  - Teacher training & recruitment
  - Digital education (DIKSHA platform, smart classrooms)
  - Inclusive education (SC/ST, girls, CWSN)
  - Vocational education at secondary level
- **New in 2.0:** EdTech integration, AI-based personalized learning, coding from Class 6

## Implementation Progress (as of May 2026)
| Parameter | Status |
|-----------|--------|
| Schools with functional toilets | ✅ 95%+ (boys & girls separate) |
| Schools with electricity | ✅ 90%+ |
| Smart classrooms | 🔄 5+ lakh classrooms equipped |
| Teacher training | ✅ 50+ lakh teachers trained through DIKSHA |
| Digital content on DIKSHA | ✅ 2+ lakh pieces of content (in 40+ languages) |

## Challenges
- **Learning outcomes** — NAS surveys show only ~50% of Class 5 students can read Class 2 text
- **Teacher quality** — 20% teacher absenteeism rate; 15% untrained teachers
- **Digital divide** — Only 25% rural households have internet; smartphone access limited
- **Infrastructure gaps** — 10%+ schools still lack functional toilets, 15% lack electricity
- **Equity gap** — Learning outcomes vary hugely across states (Kerala 80% vs. Bihar 40%)
