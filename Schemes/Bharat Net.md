# Bharat Net

## Overview
India's flagship **optical fibre network** project to connect all **2.5+ lakh Gram Panchayats** with high-speed broadband, enabling digital services in rural India — education, health, e-governance, financial inclusion.

## Key Details
- **Architecture:** Fibre-to-the-Panchayat (optical fibre to every GP office)
- **Speed:** 100 Mbps to each Gram Panchayat
- **Reach:** All 2.5+ lakh Gram Panchayats
- **Technology:** Mix of underground optical fibre, aerial fibre, radio & satellite (for difficult terrain)
- **Last mile:** Wi-Fi hotspots & fibre to villages (village-level entrepreneurs operate)
- **Digital Services enabled:** eSanjeevani telemedicine, DIKSHA education, CSCs, bank correspondents

## Implementation Progress (as of May 2026)
| Parameter | Status |
|-----------|--------|
| GPs connected (optical fibre) | ✅ 2+ lakh out of 2.5+ lakh |
| Wi-Fi hotspots operational | ✅ 1+ lakh in rural areas |
| Dark fibre lit up | 🔄 ~60% utilisation — capacity not fully used |
| Last-mile connectivity | 🔄 ~80% GPs have some last-mile connectivity |
| PM-WANI (public Wi-Fi) | 🔄 2+ lakh hotspots set up through local entrepreneurs |

## Challenges
- **Right of Way (RoW)** — State-level RoW permissions delayed fibre laying significantly
- **Vandalism & theft** — Fibre cables frequently cut/stolen in rural areas
- **Dark fibre underutilisation** — Telecom companies not leasing capacity as expected
- **Maintenance** — No dedicated O&M budget post-commissioning
- **Slow last mile** — Connecting fibre to individual villages within GP takes additional time & funds
- **Satellite dependency** — Remaining ~50,000 GPs in difficult terrain rely on satellite which is slower
