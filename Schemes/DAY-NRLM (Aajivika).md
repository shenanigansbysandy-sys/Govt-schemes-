# DAY-NRLM (Aajivika)

## Overview
India's **largest poverty alleviation & livelihood promotion program**, organizing **rural women into Self Help Groups (SHGs)** to provide access to financial services, skill training, and enterprise support.

## Key Details
- **Core Model:** Women SHGs → Village Organizations (VOs) → Cluster Level Federations (CLFs)
- **Coverage:** All districts, 6,000+ blocks, 2.5+ lakh gram panchayats
- **Components:**
  1. **Social mobilization** — SHG formation & capacity building
  2. **Financial inclusion** — Bank linkage, internal lending, community investment fund
  3. **Livelihood promotion** — Farm & non-farm enterprise development
  4. **Social inclusion** — Focus on poorest of poor (PooP families)
- **Women SHGs:** 80+ lakh SHGs with 9+ crore women members

## Challenges
- **Quality vs. quantity** — Rapid SHG expansion has diluted group quality in many states
- **Loan recovery** — Overlapping loans & multiple memberships cause repayment stress
- **Skill to income conversion** — Many women trained but unable to start viable enterprises
- **Elite capture** — Better-off women dominate SHG leadership; poorest sometimes excluded
- **Bank linkage** — Banks in some states (Bihar, UP) are reluctant to lend to SHGs without collateral
