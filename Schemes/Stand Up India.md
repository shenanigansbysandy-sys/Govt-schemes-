# Stand Up India

## Overview
A scheme to **promote entrepreneurship among SC/ST and women** by providing **bank loans (₹10 lakh to ₹1 crore)** for setting up **greenfield enterprises**.

## Key Details
- **Target:** At least 1 SC/ST borrower AND 1 woman borrower per bank branch
- **Loan Range:** ₹10 lakh to ₹1 crore
- **Collateral:** Up to ₹10 lakh — collateral-free; above ₹10 lakh — collateral as per bank norms
- **Refinance:** Small Industries Development Bank of India (SIDBI) provides refinance
- **Margin Money:** Up to 15% of project cost
- **Sectors:** Trading, manufacturing, services (any sector except prohibited list)

| Branch coverage | ✅ 1.5+ lakh branches participating |

## Challenges
- **Low awareness** — Target communities (SC/ST, women) unaware of the scheme
- **Bank reluctance** — Banks prefer lending to established businesses; hesitant for first-time entrepreneurs
- **Project viability** — Many SC/ST entrepreneurs lack business planning & financial literacy
- **Collateral demand** — Despite scheme intent, banks still demand collateral for >₹10 lakh loans
- **Regional skew** — Southern states account for 50%+ of loans; North & East lag significantly
