# Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM)

## Overview
Aims to provide **Functional Household Tap Connection (FHTC)** to **every rural household** in India with adequate quantity and prescribed quality of water.

## Current Status (May 2026)
- **~82% saturation** achieved (~15 crore rural households covered)
- Mission **extended to December 2028** for 100% coverage
- Focus on **quality-affected areas**, desert regions, and water-scarce areas

## Key Features
- **55 litres per person per day** minimum supply
- **Water quality monitoring** at source and household level
- **Greywater management** & reuse
- **Community involvement** through Village Water & Sanitation Committees (VWSC)
