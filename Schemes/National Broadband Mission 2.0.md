# National Broadband Mission 2.0 (NBM 2.0)

## Overview
An accelerated mission to provide **high-speed broadband connectivity** to every Indian citizen, building on Bharat Net's fibre infrastructure and adding **5G, satellite broadband, and Wi-Fi** to achieve universal connectivity.

## Key Targets (NBM 2.0)
| Parameter | Target | Current |
|-----------|--------|---------|
| Broadband in all villages | Connect 100% of villages | ~80% covered |
| 5G coverage | 100% population by 2027 | 90% districts covered |
| Fixed-line broadband | 50% of households | ~15% |
| Internet speed (rural) | 100 Mbps to all GPs | 50 Mbps avg |
| Public Wi-Fi hotspots | 5+ lakh by 2027 | 2+ lakh |
| Satellite broadband | 100% coverage incl. remote areas | Pilot phase |

## Key Interventions
- **Right of Way (RoW) reforms** — Simplified permissions for fibre & tower installation
- **Submarine cable connectivity** — New cables for Andaman, Lakshadweep, NE
- **Satellite broadband** — Gateway & user terminal manufacturing in India
- **Fiberisation** — 10 lakh km additional fibre in 3 years
- **In-filling Bharat Net** — Last-mile connectivity through VLEs & PM-WANI

## Implementation Progress (as of May 2026)
| Parameter | Status |
|-----------|--------|
| Fiber laid (NBM 2.0) | 🔄 2+ lakh km new fibre laid |
| 5G coverage | ✅ 90%+ districts — extended|
| Satellite broadband licenses | ✅ 3 private operators (Starlink, Eutelsat, Jio-SES) |
| RoW portal | ✅ Digital single-window — 20+ states onboarded |
| PM-WANI hotspots | 🔄 2+ lakh Wi-Fi hotspots operational |

## Challenges
- **RoW still problematic** — Despite reforms, states & local bodies still create barriers
- **Spectrum pricing** — 5G spectrum auction pricing remains high; inhibits investment
- **Optical fibre gap** — India has only 0.8 fibre-km per capita vs. 2+ in China & developed nations
- **Affordability** — Fixed broadband costs are high relative to mobile data (India cheapest globally)
- **Digital literacy** — Internet availability doesn't guarantee usage; 40%+ rural population not online
- **Right-of-way fees** — Some states charge arbitrary fees for fibre & tower installation
