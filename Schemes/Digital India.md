# Digital India

## Overview
Flagship programme to transform India into a **digitally empowered society and knowledge economy**.

## Digital India 2.0 (2025)
- **India AI Mission** — Deep-tech & AI-based governance in local languages
- **Near-total 5G rural district coverage**
- **Focus on cybersecurity** and digital sovereignty

## Key Pillars
| Pillar | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Digital Infrastructure** | High-speed internet, DigiLocker, UMANG, Aadhaar |
| **Digital Services** | 300+ services on mobile, DBT, e-governance |
| **Digital Literacy** | PMGDISHA — digital literacy for rural India |
| **Digital Manufacturing** | Electronics manufacturing & PLI for IT hardware |

## Key Outcomes
- **400M+ DigiLocker users**
- **1.3B+ Aadhaar enrolments**
- **50B+ digital transactions** annually
- **UMANG:** 300+ government services in one app
