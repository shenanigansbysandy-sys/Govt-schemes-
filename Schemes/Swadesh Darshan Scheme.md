# Swadesh Darshan Scheme

## Overview
India's flagship tourism scheme for **integrated development of theme-based tourist circuits** across the country, enhancing tourist infrastructure, connectivity, and experience at destinations.

## Key Details (Swadesh Darshan 2.0)
- **Approach:** Destination-centric (rather than circuit-based earlier)
- **Focus:** Sustainable & responsible tourism
- **Themes (1.0):** Eco-tourism, Heritage, Spiritual, Jungle, Beach, Culture, etc.
- **Previously identified circuits (1.0):** 15 theme-based circuits (Ramayana Circuit, Buddhist Circuit, North East Circuit, etc.)
- **Swadesh Darshan 2.0 (2023 onwards):** 
  - 100+ destinations selected for holistic development
  - Public-private partnership model
  - Focus on wellness tourism, MICE tourism, adventure tourism

## Challenges
- **Slow implementation** — Average 3-5 years for circuit development; much slower than planned
- **Land & clearance issues** — Forest, coastal, and heritage clearances cause delays
- **State cooperation** — Tourism development requires state land, policy support, and maintenance
- **Maintenance deficit** — Many developed circuits deteriorate quickly without O&M budget
- **Overtourism vs. undertourism** — Some destinations (Goa, Agra) are overcrowded while others lack visitors
- **Safety perception** — India's tourism growth hampered by safety concerns for foreign tourists
