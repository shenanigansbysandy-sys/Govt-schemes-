# Saksham Anganwadi & Poshan 2.0

## Overview
A revamped nutrition & early childhood development program announced in **Budget 2025-26**, consolidating & strengthening the **Anganwadi network** (13.5+ lakh centres) with enhanced nutrition norms, digital tracking, and infrastructure upgrade.

## Key Details
- **Target Beneficiaries:** ~8 crore children (0-6 yrs), 1 crore pregnant & lactating mothers, 20 lakh adolescent girls
- **Upgraded Components:**
  1. **Improved Nutrition:** Fortified take-home rations, micronutrient supplements, millet-based meals
  2. **Technology:** CAS-based digital growth monitoring (real-time malnutrition tracking)
  3. **Infrastructure:** Smart Anganwadis with water, electricity, toilets, play equipment
  4. **Early Childhood Education:** Structured pre-school curriculum (NEP 2020 alignment)
  5. **Adolescent Girls Focus:** Nutrition, health, life skills, digital literacy
  6. **Poshan Maah (Sept):** Intensified month-long nutrition awareness campaigns

## Challenges
- **Anganwadi Worker underpayment** — Workers get ₹4,500-₹7,500/month honorarium; many states supplement
- **Infrastructure gap** — Majority of Aanganwadi centres in rented/dilapidated buildings without basic amenities
- **Supply chain** — Take-home rations often delayed, adulterated, or of poor quality
- **Last-mile malnutrition** — India's wasting rate (children under 5) still ~18% — reduction slow
- **Worker motivation** — Overworked, underpaid Anganwadi workers are demotivated; high attrition
