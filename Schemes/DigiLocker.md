# DigiLocker

## Overview
India's flagship **digital document wallet** platform that issues **e-documents** in real-time from government agencies & allows citizens to **store, share, and verify** documents digitally — eliminating the need for physical documents.

## Key Details
- **Registered Users:** 400+ million
- **Issued Documents:** 8+ billion (from 2,200+ issuer agencies)
- **Documents Available:** Aadhaar, PAN, Driving License, RC, Marksheets, Voter ID, Caste Certificate, Income Certificate, Property Documents, Passport, etc.
- **Verification:** QR code-based tamper-proof digital verification
- **Integration:** Linked with UMANG, MyScheme, PM-KISAN, Ayushman Bharat
- **Storage:** 1 GB free encrypted storage per user

## Challenges
- **Digital divide** — 50%+ of Indians lack smartphones to use DigiLocker
- **Aadhaar linkage dependency** — Non-Aadhaar holders face difficulty in account creation
- **Awareness** — Many citizens still carry physical documents unnecessarily
- **Storage limits** — 1 GB free storage insufficient for heavy users
- **Acceptance by physical authorities** — Some government offices & private entities still demand physical copies
