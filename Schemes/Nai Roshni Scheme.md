# Nai Roshni Scheme

## Overview
A **leadership development scheme** for **minority community women** that provides training & exposure to empower them with knowledge, confidence, and skills to participate in **local governance, education, economic activities, and community decision-making**.

## Key Details
- **Training:** 6-day residential/non-residential training programme
- **Topics Covered:** 
  - Leadership & communication skills
  - Government schemes & entitlements
  - Health, hygiene & nutrition
  - Legal rights (women-related laws)
  - Financial literacy & digital skills
  - Life skills & decision-making
- **Eligibility:** Minority women aged 18-65 years
- **Target:** 5+ lakh women trained so far

## Implementation Progress (as of May 2026)
| Parameter | Status |
|-----------|--------|
| Women trained | ✅ 5+ lakh (cumulative) |
| States covered | ✅ 25+ states |
| Training partners | ✅ 200+ NGOs as implementing partners |
| Post-training impact | 📈 Many graduates become SHG leaders, Panchayat members, health workers |
| Community projects | 🔄 10,000+ community projects initiated by trained women |

## Challenges
- **Scale** — 5 lakh trained against a target population of 10+ crore minority women (0.5%)
- **Post-training follow-up** — No structured follow-up mechanism after the 6-day training
- **Quality variance** — Partner NGOs vary in quality; some training sessions lack depth
- **Economic empowerment gap** — Training builds awareness but doesn't create livelihoods
- **Caste & class barriers within minorities** — Better-off minority women access schemes first
