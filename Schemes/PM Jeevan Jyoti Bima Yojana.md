# PM Jeevan Jyoti Bima Yojana (PMJJBY)

## Overview
A **low-cost life insurance scheme** offering **₹2 lakh life cover** at a nominal annual premium of **₹436** (auto-debited from bank account) for individuals aged 18-50 years.

## Key Details
- **Cover:** ₹2 lakh on death of insured (any cause)
- **Premium:** ₹436 per year (auto-debit from savings bank account)
- **Eligibility:** Savings bank account holder, age 18-50 years
- **Tenure:** 1 year (renewable annually); cover till age 55
- **Enrolments:** 20+ crore (largest life insurance scheme globally by enrolment)
- **Claims settled:** ₹50,000+ crore paid to families of deceased subscribers

## Benefits
- Very low premium (₹36/month) — widely affordable
- No medical checkup required
- Auto-debit from Jan Dhan/bank account — frictionless renewal
- Can be linked with any savings account (not just Jan Dhan)

## Challenges
- **Low awareness** — Many enrolled don't know they are covered; families don't file claims
- **Auto-debit failures** — Policy lapses if bank balance insufficient (40%+ auto-debit failures)
- **Claim settlement delays** — Delayed death certificate & nominee identification
- **₹2 lakh inadequate** — Funeral costs & debt repayment may need higher cover
- **Overlapping schemes** — Confusion with PMSBY (accident cover) among subscribers
