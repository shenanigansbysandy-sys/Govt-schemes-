# eSanjeevani Programme

## Overview
India's **national telemedicine service** that enables patients to consult doctors **remotely** via video/audio, reducing the need for physical hospital visits. It operates through **Ayushman Arogya Mandirs** (hub-and-spoke model) and directly via the eSanjeevani app.

## Key Details
- **Two Models:**
  1. **eSanjeevani AB-HWC** — Doctor-to-doctor (patient at AAM → specialist) — 80% of consultations
  2. **eSanjeevani OPD** — Patient-to-doctor (directly from home via mobile)
- **Consultations:** 20+ crore total (world's largest telemedicine program)
- **Platform:** Available on web + Android/iOS app in multiple languages

## Implementation Progress (as of May 2026)
| Parameter | Status |
|-----------|--------|
| Total consultations | ✅ 20+ crore |
| Daily consultations | ✅ 1.5+ lakh/day average |
| Connected AAMs | ✅ 1.7+ lakh Ayushman Arogya Mandirs |
| Specialities available | ✅ 40+ specialities (cardiology, dermatology, psychiatry, etc.) |
| Registered doctors | ✅ 2+ lakh doctors |

## Challenges
- **Internet connectivity** — Rural broadband & 4G/5G still patchy in many areas
- **Digital literacy** — Elderly patients struggle with app-based consultation
- **Quality of consultation** — Remote diagnosis has limitations (no physical examination)
- **Prescription & follow-up** — Ensuring follow-up compliance after tele-consultation
- **Doctor availability** — Shortage of specialists online during peak hours
