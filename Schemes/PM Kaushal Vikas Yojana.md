# PM Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY)

## Overview
Flagship skill development scheme providing **skill training and certification** to Indian youth with monetary rewards and placement support.

## Components
| Component | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Short Term Training | 2-6 months, industry-linked courses |
| Recognition of Prior Learning | Certification for existing workers |
| Special Projects | Customized training for specific groups |
| Placement Support | Job placement assistance post-training |

## Key Stats
- **Millions trained** across job roles aligned with NSQF
- Training centres across all districts
- Focus on **Industry 4.0**, green skills, healthcare
