# PM Gati Shakti National Master Plan

## Overview
A digital platform connecting **16 infrastructure ministries** for coordinated planning, design, and execution of infrastructure projects to reduce logistics costs.

## Key Objectives
- Reduce **logistics cost** from 14% of GDP to <10%
- **Integrated planning** of road, rail, air, waterways, and telecom
- **GIS-based master planning** for 200+ greenfield projects
- **Multi-modal connectivity** for industrial zones & economic corridors

## Progress
- Over **1,400+ projects** tracked on Gati Shakti portal
- Integration with **PM Gram Sadak Yojana**, Bharatmala, Sagarmala
- **National Logistics Policy (2022)** operationalized through this platform
