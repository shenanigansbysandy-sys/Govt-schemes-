# PM Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)

## Overview
Crop insurance scheme providing **affordable insurance coverage** to farmers against crop loss/damage due to natural calamities, pests, and diseases.

## Coverage
- All food crops (cereals, millets, pulses)
- Oilseeds
- Annual commercial/horticultural crops

## Premium Rates
| Season | Premium (% of Sum Insured) |
|--------|---------------------------|
| Kharif | Up to 2% |
| Rabi | Up to 1.5% |
| Commercial/Horticultural | Up to 5% |

✅ **Balance premium subsidized** by Central & State Governments (shared)

## Key Features
- **Total sum insured at actuarial rates**
- **Coverage from pre-sowing to post-harvest**
- **GPS-based crop cutting experiments** for accurate assessment
- **Direct claim settlement** in bank accounts
