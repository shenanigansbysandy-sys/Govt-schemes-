# Startup India

## Overview
Government's flagship initiative to **build a strong ecosystem for nurturing innovation and startups**, leading to sustainable economic growth and large-scale employment.

## Startup India 2.0 (2026)
- **₹10,000 crore deep-tech fund** for AI, robotics, quantum tech
- Celebrated **10-year milestone** in January 2026
- Focus on **manufacturing, deep-tech, and tier-2/3 cities**

## Key Benefits
- **Tax holiday** for 3 consecutive years
- **Self-certification** under 9 labour & environmental laws
- **₹10,000 crore Fund of Funds** (2025)
- **Startup India Seed Fund** for early-stage startups
- **Ease of exit** (insolvency & bankruptcy code)
- **National startup awards** and recognition

## Key Stats
- **1.5 lakh+ DPIIT-recognized startups**
- **100+ unicorns** from India
- Present in **all states & UTs**
