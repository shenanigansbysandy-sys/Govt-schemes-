# PM Mudra Yojana (PMMY)

## Overview
Provides **collateral-free loans** up to ₹20 lakh to non-corporate, non-farm small/micro entrepreneurs.

## Loan Categories
| Category | Loan Amount | Purpose |
|----------|-------------|---------|
| **Shishu** | Up to ₹50,000 | New businesses |
| **Kishor** | ₹50,001 – ₹5 lakh | Growing businesses |
| **Tarun** | ₹5,00,001 – ₹10 lakh | Established businesses |
| **Tarun Plus** 🆕 | Up to ₹20 lakh | Proven entrepreneurs (2024) |

## Eligibility
- Small entrepreneurs, artisans, food processors, service sector
- No collateral required
- No income tax return needed for small loans

## Key Stats
- **40+ crore loans** sanctioned since inception
- **Majority to women** entrepreneurs
- Extensive outreach through banks, NBFCs, MFIs
