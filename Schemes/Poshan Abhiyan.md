# Poshan Abhiyan (National Nutrition Mission)

## Overview
India's **flagship nutrition mission** aimed at reducing **stunting, undernutrition, anemia, and low birth weight** among children (0-6 yrs), adolescent girls, and women of reproductive age through targeted interventions.

## Key Targets
| Indicator | Baseline (2015-16) | Target (2025) | Current (2026) |
|-----------|-------------------|---------------|----------------|
| Stunting (under-5) | 38.4% | 25% | ~22% (NFHS-6) |
| Underweight (under-5) | 35.8% | 20% | ~18% |
| Wasting (under-5) | 21% | 15% | ~16% |
| Anemia (women 15-49) | 53% | 32% | ~40% |
| Low birth weight | 18% | 10% | ~14% |

## Key Interventions
- **ICDS strengthening** — Better take-home rations, growth monitoring
- **Community mobilization** — Poshan Maah, Poshan Pakhwada (intensive nutrition campaigns)
- **Technology** — POSHAN Tracker (real-time monitoring of Anganwadi services)
- **Convergence** — Linking health, water, sanitation, and nutrition programmes
- **Anaemia Mukt Bharat** — Iron+folic acid supplementation, deworming, fortified foods

## Implementation Progress (as of May 2026)
| Parameter | Status |
|-----------|--------|
| POSHAN Tracker app | ✅ Universal rollout — 13.5+ lakh Anganwadis |
| Anaemia Mukt Bharat | 🔄 IFA distribution improving but compliance low |
| Stunting reduction | 📈 Down from 38% to ~22% — significant progress |
| Severe acute malnutrition | 🔄 Malnutrition Treatment Centres operational in 500+ districts |
| Poshan Maah events | ✅ Annual — reaching 10+ crore households |

## Challenges
- **Anemia stubbornly high** — 40% women anemic despite 8+ years of intervention
- **Quality of take-home ration** — Irregular supply, quality issues reported in many states
- **Inter-state variation** — Bihar, UP, Jharkhand lag far behind Kerala, Tamil Nadu on all nutrition metrics
- **Anganwadi workers** — Overburdened, underpaid; ration distribution is one of many duties
- **Convergence failure** — Despite stated intent, health, nutrition, and WASH sectors still operate in silos
