# Smart Cities Mission

## Overview
An urban renewal and smart city development mission covering **100 selected cities** across India, aimed at providing **core infrastructure**, a **clean & sustainable environment**, and a **decent quality of life** through smart solutions.

## Key Details
- **Coverage:** 100 cities over 5 years (extended)
- **Core Infrastructure:** Water supply, electricity, sanitation, public transport, housing, IT connectivity, e-governance, safety
- **Approach:** Area-based development (retrofitting, redevelopment, greenfield) + Pan-city smart solutions
- **Funding:** 50:50 sharing between Centre & State (₹500-₹1,000 crore per city)

## Implementation Progress (as of May 2026)
| Parameter | Status |
|-----------|--------|
| Projects completed | ✅ 7,500+ projects out of ~8,000 completed |
| Total investment | ✅ ₹1.8+ lakh crore invested |
| Integrated Command & Control Centres (ICCC) | ✅ Operational in 90+ cities |
| Smart mobility | ✅ Smart traffic, intelligent transport systems (ITS) deployed |
| Remaining projects | 🔄 ~500 projects in final stages |

## Challenges
- **Uneven progress** — Only 20-25 cities have achieved significant transformation; others lag
- **Financial sustainability** — Many cities lack revenue streams to maintain smart systems post-project
- **Capacity deficit** — Urban local bodies lack technical & managerial capacity
- **Citizen engagement** — Low public participation in planning & feedback
- **Data privacy concerns** — Surveillance systems raise privacy questions (cameras, sensors)
