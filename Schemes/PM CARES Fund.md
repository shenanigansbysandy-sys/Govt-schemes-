# PM CARES Fund

## Overview
A **public charitable trust** set up during the COVID-19 pandemic to provide **emergency assistance** to citizens during disasters, pandemics, and other emergency situations. It supplements government resources.

## Key Details
- **Structure:** Registered as a Public Charitable Trust (not a government fund)
- **Chairperson:** Prime Minister
- **Trustees:** Defence Minister, Home Minister, Finance Minister
- **Source of Funds:** Voluntary donations (corporate & individual) — **not from government budget**
- **100% tax exemption** for donors under 80G

## Utilization (Selected)
- **COVID Response:** Oxygen plants, ventilators, vaccine procurement, pediatric ICU
- **Children affected by COVID:** PM-CARES for Children — support to orphans (education, pension)
- **Health Infrastructure:** 1,000+ oxygen plants installed at hospitals
- **Disaster Relief:** Floods, cyclones, earthquakes
- **Research:** Vaccine R&D support

## Transparency & Controversies
- ✅ Has published annual audited reports
- ❌ Not under Right to Information (RTI) — trust is not a "public authority"
- ❌ Not under CAG audit (voluntarily audited by private auditors)
- 🔴 Criticism: Lack of parliamentary oversight compared to PMNRF
