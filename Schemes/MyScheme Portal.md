# MyScheme Portal (myscheme.gov.in)

## Overview
India's **national one-stop digital platform** for discovering and applying for **government schemes** — covering **2,580+ schemes** (Central + State) across **15 categories**. Citizens enter their details once and get a **personalized list of eligible schemes**.

## Key Features
- **Single Gateway:** Access all Central & State schemes from one portal
- **Eligibility Engine:** Answer 8-12 questions → personalized scheme list
- **Browse by:** Category, State/UT, Central Ministry, Life Event
- **Scheme Details:** Benefits, eligibility, required documents, application process, FAQs
- **Multilingual:** Available in 14 Indian languages + English
- **Integration:** Linked with DigiLocker, Aadhaar, UMANG
- **Helpline:** (011) 24303714 | support-myscheme@digitalindia.gov.in

## Challenges
- **Scheme completeness** — Not all state schemes are listed; some states slow in onboarding
- **Application integration** — Currently directs to ministry portals; full e-application within platform pending
- **Real-time data** — Scheme status updates depend on ministries updating their information
- **Awareness** — Still not widely known; needs more promotional campaigns
- **Mobile app** — No dedicated mobile app yet (works via mobile browser)
- **State scheme detail depth** — State scheme pages are less detailed than Central ones
