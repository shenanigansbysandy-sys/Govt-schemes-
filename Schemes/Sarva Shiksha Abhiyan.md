# Sarva Shiksha Abhiyan (SSA)

## Overview
India's **flagship elementary education program** that operationalized the **Right to Education (RTE) Act, 2009**, aiming to provide **free & compulsory education** to all children aged 6-14 years.

## Key Achievements
| Parameter | 2001 | 2018 (when subsumed) |
|-----------|------|----------------------|
| Gross Enrolment Ratio | 82% | 100%+ |
| Dropout rate (primary) | 39% | 4% |
| Schools in India | 10 lakh | 16+ lakh |
| Teacher-pupil ratio | 1:43 | 1:23 |
| Girl enrolment | 48% | 49.6% |
| Out-of-school children | 3.2 crore | ~60 lakh |

## Challenges (Legacy)
- **Quality over quantity** — Universal enrolment achieved but learning outcomes remained poor
- **Teacher shortage** — Over 5 lakh teacher vacancies at peak
- **Infrastructure** — Many schools lacked basic amenities despite SSA funding
- **RTE implementation gaps** — Untrained teachers, no neighborhood schools in some areas
