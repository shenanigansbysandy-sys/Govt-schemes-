# iDEX (Innovations for Defence Excellence)

## Overview
A pioneering ecosystem that **funds and supports startups & MSMEs** developing innovative solutions for the Indian Armed Forces, bridging the gap between **defence requirements** and **Indian innovation**.

## Key Details
- **Objective:** Achieve self-reliance in defence technology (Atmanirbhar Bharat)
- **Structure:** iDEX serves as the execution arm — DIO (Defence Innovation Organisation) provides funding
- **Funding Stages:**
  - **Support for Prototype & Validation:** Up to ₹1.5 crore per project
  - **Development & Pilot:** Follow-on funding up to ₹10 crore
- **Key Programs:**
  1. **Dare to Dream** — Innovation contest for individuals & startups
  2. **ADITI Scheme** — Complementary deep-tech defence funding
  3. **SMART (Solution for Military Adaptation by Startups)**
- **Sectors Covered:** Drones, AI/ML, cybersecurity, robotics, advanced materials, radar, communication

## Challenges
- **Long procurement cycle** — Even after successful prototype, bulk procurement takes 2-4 years
- **Testing infrastructure** — Startups lack access to defence testing labs & ranges
- **Funding gap post-iDEX** — "Valley of death" between prototype and series A/B funding
- **Security clearances** — Startups with foreign funding face security clearance hurdles
- **IP ownership** — Unclear IP sharing agreements between startups & armed forces
- **Risk aversion** — Armed forces hesitant to bet on unproven startup technology at scale
