# Rural Prosperity & Resilience Programme

## Overview
Announced in **Budget 2025-26**, this programme aims for **multi-sectoral rural development** to tackle **underemployment** and create **sustainable livelihood opportunities** in rural India.

## Key Details
- **Objective:** Address underemployment in rural areas by linking agriculture, rural enterprise, and skill development
- **Approach:** Convergence of existing schemes (MGNREGS, DAY-NRLM, DDU-GKY) under one umbrella
- **Target:** Rural youth, small & marginal farmers, landless families
- **Focus Areas:** 
  1. Agri-processing & value addition
  2. Rural tourism & crafts
  3. Digital services & gig economy participation
  4. Green livelihoods (solar, forestry)

## Challenges
- **Implementation complexity** — Requires unprecedented coordination across 4+ ministries
- **Capacity at Gram Panchayat level** — Local bodies lack technical & planning capacity
- **Funding uncertainty** — No separate budget allocation; depends on convergence savings
- **Measuring impact** — Quantifying "underemployment" reduction is methodologically complex
- **Private sector participation** — Attracting rural enterprise investments is challenging
