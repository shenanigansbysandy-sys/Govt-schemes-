# Soil Health Card Scheme

## Overview
A flagship scheme that provides farmers with **Soil Health Cards** containing nutrient status of their soil and **customized fertilizer recommendations** to improve productivity and reduce overuse of chemical fertilizers.

## Key Details
- **Lifecycle:** Soil samples tested every 3 years for each farming household
- **Coverage:** All agricultural land across India
- **Parameters Tested:** 12 parameters including NPK, micronutrients, pH, organic carbon
- **Goal:** Promote **balanced fertilizer use** — reduce NPK overuse, correct micronutrient deficiencies

## Challenges
- **Quality of soil testing** — Many labs lack proper equipment; inconsistency in results
- **Awareness & adoption** — Only ~40% farmers actually follow recommendations on the card
- **Lab capacity** — Shortage of well-equipped soil testing labs in rural areas
- **Follow-through** — No mechanism to ensure farmers actually use the recommendations
- **Digitization gaps** — Cards not fully integrated with fertilizer subsidy system (DBT)
