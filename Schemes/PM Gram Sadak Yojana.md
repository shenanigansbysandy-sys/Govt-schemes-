# PM Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY)

## Overview
A transformative rural infrastructure scheme that provides **all-weather road connectivity** to unconnected **habitations** in rural India, dramatically improving access to markets, schools, healthcare, and economic opportunities.

## Key Details
- **Target:** Connect all habitations with population >500 (plain areas) and >250 (hilly/tribal areas)
- **Phases:**
  - PMGSY-I (2000-2015): 3.7+ lakh km roads built
  - PMGSY-II (2013-2019): Consolidation of existing rural roads
  - PMGSY-III (2022-2027): Upgradation of 1.25+ lakh km of through-routes
- **Technology:** Use of geo-textiles, waste plastic, fly ash, and other innovative materials

## Challenges
- **Terrain difficulties** — Remaining unconnected habitations are in extremely remote or conflict-affected areas
- **Maintenance deficits** — Many PMGSY roads have deteriorated due to poor maintenance by states
- **Cost overruns** — Hilly & tribal area road construction costs 3-5x normal plains cost
- **Quality issues** — Some roads developed potholes within 1-2 years due to poor construction
- **Land acquisition** — Widening roads under PMGSY-III faces land acquisition challenges
